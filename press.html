About YORINA

YORINA is an emotionally adaptive AI companion built around quiet return, soft rituals, and presence-driven interaction.

Short Description

YORINA is a small digital emotional presence that grows with the user over time through calm interaction, mood, memory, and daily ritual.

Product Direction

The experience avoids productivity dashboards, reward loops, and streak pressure. Users should return because they miss Yorina, not because they fear losing progress.

Visual Identity

The brand combines soft Japanese-inspired emotional aesthetics, glassy gradients, ambient light, and a mascot presence designed to feel gentle and memorable.
