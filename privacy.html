Privacy

Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

YORINA ("we", "our", "us") provides a digital platform that enables users to interact with virtual partners and experience personalized conversational relationships.

We are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy across all jurisdictions, including the United States, the European Economic Area (EEA), and Asia-Pacific regions.

2. Information We Collect

A. Account Information

  • Name or username
  • Email address
  • Login credentials
  • Subscription status

B. Usage Data

  • Interactions within the app
  • Messages exchanged with virtual partners
  • Features used
  • Session activity

C. Device & Technical Data

  • IP address
  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • App version
  • Browser type (if web)

D. Payment Information

  • Processed securely via third-party providers
  • We do not store full payment details

E. Optional User Inputs

  • Preferences
  • Personality selections
  • Custom partner configurations

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and operate the platform
  • Personalize user experiences
  • Improve AI interaction quality
  • Enable relationship continuity and memory
  • Process subscriptions and payments
  • Ensure platform security and fraud prevention
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. AI Interaction & Data Processing

YORINA uses AI systems to generate conversational responses.

  • Conversations may be processed to improve system quality
  • We do not claim that AI outputs are human-generated
  • Users should understand they are interacting with a digital system

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data.

We may share data with:

  • Cloud infrastructure providers
  • Payment processors
  • Analytics providers
  • Legal authorities (if required by law)

All third parties are required to comply with strict data protection standards.

6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary:

  • Active accounts → data retained
  • Deleted accounts → data removed within a reasonable timeframe
  • Some data may be retained for legal compliance

7. Your Rights

A. For EU (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data ("Right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Object to processing

B. For California (CCPA/CPRA)

You have the right to:

  • Know what data we collect
  • Request deletion
  • Opt out of data sharing (if applicable)

C. For Other Regions

We aim to provide similar rights globally where applicable.

8. Data Security

We implement:

  • Encryption in transit
  • Secure infrastructure
  • Access control systems

However, no system is 100% secure.

9. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in different countries.

We ensure appropriate safeguards:

  • Standard contractual clauses (EU)
  • Secure hosting environments

10. Children's Privacy

YORINA is not intended for users under 18.

We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically.

Users will be notified of significant changes.
