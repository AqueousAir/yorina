YORINA – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) describes how YORINA (“Company”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, processes, stores, and discloses your information when you access or use the YORINA platform (the “Service”).

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Policy.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information in the following categories:

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use YORINA, you may provide:

  • Account information (name, email, login credentials)
  • Profile data (preferences, personality selections, settings)
  • User-generated content (messages, prompts, interactions)
  • Payment information (processed via third-party providers)

1.2 AI Interaction Data

Due to the nature of the Service, we collect:

  • Conversations between you and AI partners
  • Emotional signals inferred from interactions
  • Relationship progression data
  • Memory data associated with AI partners
  • Behavioral patterns and usage signals

This data is essential to deliver:

  • Memory continuity
  • Personalization
  • AI partner evolution

1.3 Automatically Collected Data

We automatically collect:

  • Device information (device type, OS, app version)
  • Log data (IP address, timestamps, usage events)
  • Performance data (crashes, diagnostics)
  • Session activity

1.4 Cookies & Tracking (if web used)

We may use cookies or similar technologies to:

  • Maintain sessions
  • Analyze usage
  • Improve performance

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use collected data to:

  • Provide and operate the Service
  • Enable AI partner interactions and memory
  • Personalize user experience
  • Improve AI models and system performance
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Ensure security and prevent misuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. AI MEMORY & DATA PROCESSING

YORINA includes memory systems that:

  • Store conversation context
  • Retain relationship history
  • Track behavioral patterns

Important:

  • Memory retention varies by subscription plan
  • Memory may be deleted, modified, or expire
  • AI memory is not guaranteed to be complete or accurate

We process this data to maintain:

  • Continuity of interaction
  • Identity persistence of AI partners

4. LEGAL BASIS FOR PROCESSING (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Contractual necessity (to provide the Service)
  • Legitimate interests (improving AI and platform performance)
  • Consent (where required, such as optional features)
  • Legal compliance

We do not sell your personal data.

5.1 Service Providers

These parties process data on our behalf under strict obligations.

5.2 Legal Requirements

We retain your data:
